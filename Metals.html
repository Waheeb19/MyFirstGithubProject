Brief History

Many of the popular metal bands of different sub-genres shown in the image below

Metal music was introduced back in the late 1960s and early 1970s. This was when Led Zeppelin, Black Sabbath and Deep Purple were founded. At the time, the genre of metal music was mainly popular in the US and the United Kingdom. Some of the common conventions of Metal music include extended guitar solos, massive sounds and is associated with aggression mainly.

Brief History

Many of the popular rock bands of different sub-genres shown in the image below

Rock music was introduced as Rock and Roll during the 1940s and 1950s and was heavily influenced by other genres such as Jazz, electric Blues and folk music. From the 60s onwards, the genre became more diverse mainly in the United States and the United Kingdom. This was when subgenres like country rock and folk rock had emerged. Various themes are also expressed in these subgenres such as romantic love and social concerns. This has also lead to cultural and social movements which in turn lead to major subcultures. However, there has been a slow decline in this genre ever since the 2000s with hip hop being the most popular genre in the United States by 2017.
